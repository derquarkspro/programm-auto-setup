How to install Chrome Driver

1. Check your Chrome version!

Got to Help > About Google Chrome or paste chrome://settings/help in your Chrome browser's address bar!

2. Download ChromeDriver for your Chrome version!

Therefore, please click the link below and save the file in your downloads directory!

Unzip the chromedrive.exe to C:\Program Files (x86)\Google\Chrome.

https://sites.google.com/a/chromium.org/chromedriver/downloads
